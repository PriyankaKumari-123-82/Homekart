About HomeKart



HomeKart is a home-based product marketplace built with the vision of empowering small local outlets to reach a wider audience. We believe that every small seller deserves the opportunity to showcase their unique products and connect with consumers who truly value home-grown creativity and authenticity.

At its core, HomeKart is more than just a shopping platform — it is a bridge between passionate sellers and conscious consumers. Whether you are a craftsperson, a home-run kitchen, or a neighborhood boutique, HomeKart gives you the tools and visibility to grow your business online.

We tailor our platform to meet the needs of both sellers and buyers, making it simple, secure, and seamless to explore, buy, and sell home-based goods. With HomeKart, we’re bringing communities closer through local products and personalized shopping experiences.

Join us in building a thriving digital bazaar where small businesses shine and customers find products made with heart.
